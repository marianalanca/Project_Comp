FunctionsAndDeclarations −> (FunctionDefinition | FunctionDeclaration | Declaration) optFuncAndDec $ 

optFuncAndDec -> FunctionsAndDeclarations | \Epsilon 

  

FunctionDefinition −> TypeSpec FunctionDeclarator FunctionBody 

 

FunctionBody −> LBRACE DeclarationsAndStatements RBRACE  

DeclarationsAndStatements −> Statement DeclarationsAndStatements | Declaration DeclarationsAndStatements | Statement | Declaration | \Epsilon 

FunctionDeclaration −> TypeSpec FunctionDeclarator SEMI 

  

FunctionDeclarator −> ID LPAR ParameterList RPAR 

  

ParameterList −> ParameterDeclaration optParamList 

optParamList -> COMMA ParameterDeclaration optParamList | \Epsilon 

  

ParameterDeclaration −> TypeSpec optParamDec 

optParamDec -> ID | \Epsilon 

  

Declaration −> TypeSpec Declarator optDeclaration SEMI 

optDeclaration -> COMMA Declarator optDeclaration | \Epsilon 

  

TypeSpec −> CHAR | INT | VOID | SHORT | DOUBLE 

  

Declarator −> ID OptDeclarator 

OptDeclarator -> ASSIGN Expr | \Epsilon 

  

Statement−> optExp SEMI 

    | LBRACE optState RBRACE 

    | IF LPAR Expr RPAR Statement optElse 

    | WHILE LPAR Expr RPAR Statement 

    | RETURN optExp SEMI 

     
	 
optExp -> Expr | \Epsilon 

optState -> Statement optState | \Epsilon 

optElse -> ELSE Statement | \Epsilon 



Expr−> Expr optExpr Expr 

    |(PLUS | MINUS | NOT) Expr 

    | ID optID | INTLIT | CHRLIT | REALLIT | LPAR Expr RPAR   

 

optExpr -> (ASSIGN | COMMA)  

    | (PLUS | MINUS | MUL | DIV | MOD)  

    | (OR | AND | BITWISEAND | BITWISEOR | BITWISEXOR)  

    | (EQ | NE | LE | GE | LT | GT)  

 

optID -> LPAR optExpCExp RPAR   | \Epsilon 

optExpCExp -> Expr optCExp | \Epsilon 

optCExp -> COMMA Expr optCExp | \Epsilon
